# Quick Start Guide

Get started with RingToneMaker in 5 easy steps!

## 1. Select Your Song 🎵

Tap **"Select Song from Library"** and choose any downloaded song from your music library.

> **Note:** DRM-protected or cloud-only songs won't work. Make sure the song is downloaded to your device.

## 2. Explore the Waveform 📊

Once loaded, you'll see a visual representation of your song:
- **Blue bars** = Selected portion (will become your ringtone)
- **Gray bars** = Unselected portion

### Interactive Controls:
- 🤏 **Pinch** to zoom in/out (up to 3x)
- 👆 **Drag** to pan left/right when zoomed
- Current zoom level shown in top-right corner

## 3. Set Your Time Range ⏱️

Choose which part of the song to use (max 30 seconds):

### Three Ways to Adjust:
1. **Text Fields** - Type exact times in seconds
2. **Steppers** (+/-) - Adjust in 0.5 second increments  
3. **Sliders** - Drag for smooth, continuous control

All three methods sync automatically!

### Tips:
- Start time must be before end time
- Maximum duration: 30 seconds
- The app will auto-adjust if you exceed limits

## 4. Preview Your Selection 🎧

Tap the **"Preview"** button to hear your selected portion.
- Button turns red and shows "Stop" while playing
- Automatically stops at the end time
- Tap "Stop" to end early

## 5. Create & Share 📤

### Export Your Ringtone:
1. Tap **"Trim & Export Ringtone"**
2. Wait for the progress indicator (usually a few seconds)
3. Success! You'll see a green confirmation

### Share Your Ringtone:
Tap **"Share as Ringtone"** to:
- 📁 Save to Files app
- ✈️ AirDrop to your Mac
- 📧 Email to yourself
- 💬 Send via Messages

### Additional Options:
- **Open in Files** - Quick access to the file location
- **Delete** - Remove the exported file

## Common Issues & Solutions

### "Cannot access this song"
**Problem:** Song is DRM-protected or cloud-only  
**Solution:** Download the song to your device first, or choose a different song

### "Permission Required"
**Problem:** App doesn't have media library access  
**Solution:** Go to Settings > Privacy > Media & Apple Music > RingToneMaker > Enable

### "Duration Adjusted"
**Problem:** Selected range exceeds 30 seconds  
**Solution:** This is automatic - the app adjusted your times to fit the limit

### "Export Failed"
**Problem:** Could not create ringtone file  
**Solution:** Check available storage space and try again

### Ringtone doesn't appear in Settings
**Problem:** M4R file needs to be synced via computer  
**Solution:** See [RINGTONE_INSTALLATION.md](RINGTONE_INSTALLATION.md) for detailed instructions

## Pro Tips 💡

1. **Find the Best Part**
   - Use zoom to see fine details in the waveform
   - Look for the chorus or hook of the song
   - Avoid silent sections at the start/end

2. **Precise Timing**
   - Use text fields for exact times
   - Use sliders for quick browsing
   - Preview multiple times to get it perfect

3. **Optimal Length**
   - 15-20 seconds is ideal for most ringtones
   - Too short = might miss calls
   - Too long = uses more storage

4. **Batch Creation**
   - Create multiple ringtones for different contacts
   - Each export gets a unique filename with timestamp
   - Old files can be deleted to save space

5. **Quality Check**
   - Always preview before exporting
   - Check that the selection sounds good
   - Make sure there's no clipping or distortion
