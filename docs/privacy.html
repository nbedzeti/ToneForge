Privacy Policy for ToneForge Studio

Last updated: January 24, 2025

1. Introduction

ToneForge Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Audio Files: Songs and audio files you select to create ringtones (stored locally on your device only)
  • Ringtone Names: Names you give to saved ringtones (stored locally on your device only)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and app version for analytics and crash reporting
  • Usage Data: Number of ringtones created, features used (stored locally)
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to serve personalized ads (only if you consent to tracking)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple's App Store
  • Display relevant advertisements (free version only)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with the app

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your ringtones and settings are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload your audio files or ringtones to any server
  • Security: Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features
  • No Account Required: We don't collect usernames, passwords, or email addresses

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible to you
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Opt-Out: Disable ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Control: Manage app permissions in iOS Settings → ToneForge Studio

7. Children's Privacy

ToneForge Studio is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

  • Local data (ringtones, settings) is retained until you delete the app
  • Analytics data is retained for 90 days
  • Ad interaction data is managed by Google AdMob (see their policy)

9. International Users

ToneForge Studio is available worldwide. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Notifying you through the app (for significant changes)

11. Your Consent

By using ToneForge Studio, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
